Keychains: A Personal Entrepreneurial Venture

In early 2019, I started to familiarize myself with the lasercutter machines at UC Berkeley and using the software, Adobe Illustrator, as my design software. I began to make keychains out of wood and acrylic, and used custom designs as well as designs of K-pop group logos and characters. Before long, I was selling these keychains online, at concerts, and through word of mouth. Unfortunately, since I no longer attend UC Berkeley, I do not have access to the lasercutter.
